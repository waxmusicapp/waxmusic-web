Privacy Policy

Effective May 2026

Wax is a music ranking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've tried to keep it short and plain English — if anything is unclear, email hello@waxmusic.app.

What we collect

To give you an account and let you rank albums, we collect:

  • Your email address — used to sign you in and contact you about your account.
  • Your display name and username — shown on your public profile.
  • The albums you rank, your ratings, and any notes you write — the core content of your account; public to other Wax users by default.
  • Your profile photo — if you choose to upload one.
  • Likes, comments, and follows you create within the app.

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the basic profile information you authorize during sign-in (typically an email address and, where you allow it, a display name). When Apple's private-relay option is enabled, we never see your real email.

How we use it

We use your information solely to provide Wax — to sign you in, save your rankings, show your profile to people you follow, and let you discover other people's lists. We do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not show ads.

Third-party services

Wax relies on a small number of external services to operate:

  • Spotify Web API — used to search albums and fetch cover art. We never share your personal information with Spotify; only the album search terms you enter are sent.
  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your account, rankings, and profile data are stored on Supabase infrastructure.
  • Last.fm API — used to suggest similar artists and albums in the Discover and Suggested tabs. Only album and artist titles are queried; no personal information is shared.

Your rights and choices

You can edit your profile, change your photo, or remove individual rankings at any time from within the app.

You can delete your account and all associated data — rankings, queue, follows, likes, comments, and profile information — from Settings → App → Delete account. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

If you'd prefer to make a data request by email instead, contact hello@waxmusic.app from the address associated with your account and we'll handle it manually.

Children

Wax is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the app. Your continued use of Wax after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests: hello@waxmusic.app.
